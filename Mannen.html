HIKING MANNEN

The living mountain

About

NO: Det kan være lurt å sette seg inn i værmeldingen før man setter ut på tur opp Møre og Romsdals høyeste topp. Det kan blåse mye på toppen og det anbefales å ha med klær etter vær. Ikke glem fjellvettregelen nr.10: "Det er ingen skam å snu".

EN: You may want to familiarize yourself with the weather forecast before setting out for a ride up Møre and Romsdal's highest peak. It can blow a lot at the top and it is recommended to bring clothes after the weather. Don't forget mountain rule nr. 10: "It's no shame to turn around".

GER: Sie können sich mit der Wettervorhersage vertraut machen, bevor Sie den höchsten Berg von Møre und Romsdal besteigen. Es kann oben viel weht und es wird empfohlen, Kleidung nach dem Wetter mitzubringen. Vergessen Sie nicht die Regel # 10: "Es ist keine Schande, sich umzudrehen".

ABOUT

You have probably heard about the man falling in the Norwegian news but this man is actually a mountain. Even tho this mountain is on the hiking list you should definitely NOT take a hike on this top. This is just a mountain to admire from the road while driving to the other mountains.
